DigiKhata is a popular Android app from Pakistan designed as a digital ledger (khata) for small businesses, focusing on customer credit (udhar), payments, inventory, bills, and reports. It's tailored for shopkeepers with Urdu/English support and cloud backup, making it ideal for cloning as a comprehensive accounting tool.

Section 12: Database & Data Relationships
Entities: Customer, Transaction (linked to customer), Product, Bill (customer+items), StockTxn

Relationships: 1:N Customer→Txn/Bill, Bill→Products

Delete: Archive, recalcs balances

Balances: Running sum stored per customer

Section 13: Performance & Edge Cases
500+ customers: Smooth lists, search indexed

10k txns: Pagination, fast ledger load

Insufficient stock: Warning popup, blocks out

Delete customer: Moves to archive, txns preserved

Search: Full-text, <1s on 10k

Errors: "Insufficient stock", "Network error - offline mode"
